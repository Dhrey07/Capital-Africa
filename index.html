WHO ARE WE

We are
Capital Africa Hub

Capital is any asset set aside to create wealth, be that money, equipment, buildings  or people. When the average person thinks of raising capital, they often think of sourcing cash, or equipment. Seldom is the term ‘capital raise’ or ‘Capital Acquisition’ associated with getting the talents that fits organizations’ technical and cultural needs. The not so funny  Irony of this reality is, however, that companies are only as good as the people running them. A company could garner all the financial resources in the world and still fail if the people pulling its strings are not skilled enough to meet its technical and cultural vision.

At Capital Africa Hub,

We know that no  organization can grow beyond the talents within its system. Therefore, we advocate that organizations across the world and especially in Africa must place a great deal of priority on finding and managing the right talents. In our quest to make this position an easy and practical one, we help our partners manage their human capital  end-to-end, thus we offer five cardinal services; Capital Plan, Capital Acquisition, Portfolio/Capital  Management, Capital Development, Capital Bonding.
